I

AI as Capital Allocator

The cost of a wrong allocation decision compounds faster than the cost of building the system that prevents it. ML models that learn portfolio-level risk structures will outperform heuristic-based allocation within 5 years across most asset classes.

II

RL in Infrastructure Optimization

Reinforcement learning applied to real-time resource management — compute scheduling, energy grids, logistics routing — creates value not by predicting outcomes, but by continuously narrowing the space of suboptimal decisions.

Autonomous Systems & Value Creation
